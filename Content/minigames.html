Free For All

Dropper

Fall down through randomly generated rings from the world height limit to the bottom of the world.

Rules

  • Weave your way through the rings. You cannot go around the rings.
  • To win the game, you must be the first player to reach the bottom.

Tips

  • If you die, you start back up at the top.
  • For players that do not reach the bottom, the winner is chosen between whoever makes it the farthest.

Monster Shoot

Shoot monsters falling from the sky. Each monster is worth different points.

Rules

  • The point values are as follows:
  • Zombie: 1 Point
  • Creeper: 2 Points
  • Skeleton: 3 Points
  • Silverfish: 5 Points

Tips

  • The game ends after 60 seconds. Earn the most points to win!

Cave Parkour

Rules

  • Reach the end of the course first to win or have the most checkpoints completed when the game ends.

Tips

  • There are 15 total checkpoints. Drop flag to reset to checkpoint.

  • First person to finish 2 laps wins!

Rainbow Run

Agility test. Collide with moving blocks and earn more points the higher up the rainbow you collide with them.

Rules

  • Black blocks will spawn on the white and move down the rainbow.
  • Colliding with the black blocks will earn you points.
  • You will earn more points the higher up the rainbow you are (closer to the white).
  • You will also earn bonus points for maintaining a streak. The streak bonus caps at 10.

Tips

  • The blocks move faster when they are higher up the rainbow.
  • The blocks spawn more frequently as the game progresses.
  • The game ends after 90 seconds!

Tips

Runner

The ground disappears as you run over it. Stay in the game the longest.

Rules

  • As you run, the blocks below you will disappear.
  • Run and jump to avoid falling to your death.
  • Last player alive wins!

Tips

  • A falling block will kill any player that it comes in contact with.
  • The falling blocks will also destroy the ground beneath it
  • If you walk or crouch at any point, you will be eliminated.

Horse Race

Race using horses. Complete checkpoints and avoid the lava and fire.

Rules

  • Go through the course while jumping over the obstacles.
  • Hold down jump key (spacebar by default) to charge up your jump. Look at xp bar to see how much your jump is charged up.
  • Run over gold blocks with your horse to reach checkpoints.
  • Be the first to complete two laps to win!

Tips

  • If your horses catches on fire, then you reset to your last checkpoint.
  • You can drop your flag at any time to reset to your checkpoint.

Rules

Tips

  • Beetroots and Nether Wart - 1 Point

Port Priority

Scavenge for three items across the map in order.

Rules

  • Each player will be given a key and be prompted with a random item to scavenge for.
  • The item will be contained in a chest at one of the seven beacons.
  • The key given will only be able to open that chest.
  • Upon finding the item, the player will be given a new key and prompted for a new item to look for.
  • The player that finds all three items first wins.

Tips

  • All players will be tasked with finding the same three items, but the order will vary for each player.
  • Chests are marked with item frames that show the item contained.
  • Use this information to remember the item corresponding to any previous beacons that you have visited.

Rules

  • Once you land on the ground, you are finished.

Tips

Memory Lane

Memorize a canvas of 15 blocks and place the blocks in the correct spot as the original.

Rules

  • After the viewing time is up, you will be given a random ore in your hand.
  • Press the button on your canvas to match the spot of the ore.
  • If you are correct, the ore will show on your canvas, and you will be given a new ore.
  • Whoever fills their canvas back first wins!

Tips

  • If you are incorrect, you will get a four-second penalty then get a few seconds to view the original canvas again.
  • At any point when viewing the original model canvas, you may press your jump key to bring yourself to your canvas.
