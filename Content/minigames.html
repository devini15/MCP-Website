Minigames

Free For All

Dropper

Fall down through randomly generated rings from the world height limit to the bottom of the world.

Rules

  • Weave your way through the rings. You cannot go around the rings.
  • To win the game, you must be the first player to reach the bottom.

Tips

  • If you die, you start back up at the top.
  • For players that do not reach the bottom, the winner is chosen between whoever makes it the farthest.

Monster Shoot

Shoot monsters falling from the sky. Each monster is worth different points.

Rules

  • The point values are as follows:
  • Zombie: 1 Point
  • Creeper: 2 Points
  • Skeleton: 3 Points
  • Silverfish: 5 Points

Tips

  • The game ends after 60 seconds. Earn the most points to win!

Cave Parkour

Parkour through 15 checkpoints in an abandoned cave with a randomly chosen path.

Rules

  • Step over redstone lamps to reach checkpoints.
  • Reach the end of the course first to win or have the most checkpoints completed when the game ends.

Tips

  • There are 15 total checkpoints. Drop flag to reset to checkpoint.

Raft Racers

Complete two through an ice and water course on a raft. Six different paths possible.

Rules

  • You will go through different ice curves, launch pads, bubble columns, and water.

Tips

  • Drop flag to bring your boat to you if your boat gets stuck.
  • First person to finish 2 laps wins!

Knockout

Punch players out of the map with a knockback stick.

Rules

  • Knock each other off of the platforms.
  • Each player has 3 lives. Last player alive wins!

Tips

  • Knocking a player off earns you a knockback 2 stick.

Hoopin' n' Swoopin'

Using an elytra, fly as far as you can through randomly generated rings

Rules

  • The course moves in a large counter-clockwise spiral going inwards.
  • The game ends once all players are on the bottom.

Tips

  • The rings get smaller as the course goes on.
  • You get one attempt. The further you make it through the course, the more points you earn.

Spleef

Dig ground and shoot blocks beneath other players to get them to fall off.

Rules

  • Spleef each other off the map.
  • The last player alive wins!

Meat Slap

Tag! Stay tagged for the least amount of total time possible.

Rules

  • Be it for the least amount of time to win the game.

Tips

  • A tag only counts if it is a full hit.

Gladiators

All players fight to the death to be the last one standing.

Rules

  • Fight other players in one massive brawl!

Tedious Tasks

Complete your 8 tasks before the other players do. You can also grief others to slow them down.

Rules

  • Complete the 8 tasks as quickly as you can.
  • Tasks will be given (in chat) in a random order for all players.

Tips

  • You can kill/disrupt other players. Do whatever it takes!
  • Make sure you have your sounds at an audible level for this game.

Tile Capture

Capture the most land possible by cutting players off. The tile color changes beneath you.

Rules

  • The players with the most tiles captured win!

Tips

  • You cannot steal nor step over tiles from other players.

Rainbow Run

Agility test. Collide with moving blocks and earn more points the higher up the rainbow you collide with them.

Rules

  • Black blocks will spawn on the white and move down the rainbow.
  • Colliding with the black blocks will earn you points.
  • You will earn more points the higher up the rainbow you are (closer to the white).
  • You will also earn bonus points for maintaining a streak. The streak bonus caps at 10.

Tips

  • The blocks move faster when they are higher up the rainbow.
  • The blocks spawn more frequently as the game progresses.
  • The game ends after 90 seconds!

Platforms

Choose one of three platforms to jump on to score points but get nothing if another player chooses the same platform as you.

Rules

  • Win the diamonds on the platforms by selecting that platform.
  • Earn the most diamonds to win!

Tips

  • If another player chooses the same platform, then neither of you win the diamonds.

Runner

The ground disappears as you run over it. Stay in the game the longest.

Rules

  • As you run, the blocks below you will disappear.
  • Run and jump to avoid falling to your death.
  • Last player alive wins!

Tips

  • A falling block will kill any player that it comes in contact with.
  • The falling blocks will also destroy the ground beneath it
  • If you walk or crouch at any point, you will be eliminated.

Horse Race

Race using horses. Complete checkpoints and avoid the lava and fire.

Rules

  • Go through the course while jumping over the obstacles.
  • Hold down jump key (spacebar by default) to charge up your jump. Look at xp bar to see how much your jump is charged up.
  • Run over gold blocks with your horse to reach checkpoints.
  • Be the first to complete two laps to win!

Tips

  • If your horses catches on fire, then you reset to your last checkpoint.
  • You can drop your flag at any time to reset to your checkpoint.

Speed Builders

Copy 4 randomly chosen designs the fastest.

Rules

  • A design will spawn on the one of the white squares.
  • Copy the build on to the other concrete square.
  • First player to copy 4 builds wins.

Tips

  • DO NOT MIRROR THE BUILD!
  • If you get stuck, you can push the button to reset your build.

Steve Says

Parody of Simon Says. Do what Steve tells you to do or get eliminated.

Rules

  • You must do the action in chat only if Steve says so.
  • If you do an action that you are not supposed to, then you get kicked out of the game!
  • The game ends after 22 actions. Earn the most points to win!

Tips

  • You earn 3 Points for completing each action on time, 4 Points for completing the action first and 0 Points for not completing the action at all.

Power Towers

Spam mash to build the highest possible tower in 10 seconds.

Rules

  • Tune your noteblock as rapidly as possible for 10 seconds.
  • Whoever mashes their noteblock the most within 10 seconds wins!

Red Light, Green Light

Move during green light. Stop during red light or get set back to the start.

Rules

  • When the light turns green, you may go forward.
  • You must stop immediately when the light turns red or else you get sent back to the start.
  • Be the first player to reach the end to win!

Tips

  • The color of the light will be shown in the chat.

Farming Frenzy

Harvest the most crops in a given time period and be on the lookout for seasonal crops.

Rules

  • Harvest as many crops as possible within 90 seconds.
  • You receive points for harvesting crops.
  • The player with the most points wins!

Tips

  • The point values for the crops are as follows:
  • Beetroots and Nether Wart - 1 Point
  • Wheat, Potatoes, and Carrots - 2 Points
  • Cocoa Beans - 4 Points
  • Melons and Pumpkins - 6 Points
  • A new crop season will occur every 10 seconds where that crop's yield is doubled.

Port Priority

Scavenge for three items across the map in order.

Rules

  • Each player will be given a key and be prompted with a random item to scavenge for.
  • The item will be contained in a chest at one of the seven beacons.
  • The key given will only be able to open that chest.
  • Upon finding the item, the player will be given a new key and prompted for a new item to look for.
  • The player that finds all three items first wins.

Tips

  • All players will be tasked with finding the same three items, but the order will vary for each player.
  • Chests are marked with item frames that show the item contained.
  • Use this information to remember the item corresponding to any previous beacons that you have visited.

Match Me Not

Select a unique item from the other players to advance yourself.

Rules

  • If you choose a material that nobody else chooses, your platform raises by one block.
  • If someone else chooses the same material as you, neither of your platforms gets raised.
  • The player with the highest platform after 8 rounds wins!

Rocket Launch

Fly the farthest distance with an elytra and a single rocket.

Rules

  • You must jump off the platform in the indicated direction and use your rocket to boost yourself as far as possible.
  • Once you land on the ground, you are finished.
  • The player that travels the farthest wins!

Tips

  • The field ends at 2160 blocks. Any extra height gained at the finish will add on to your score with a tiebreaker being who landed first.

Memory Lane

Memorize a canvas of 15 blocks and place the blocks in the correct spot as the original.

Rules

  • After the viewing time is up, you will be given a random ore in your hand.
  • Press the button on your canvas to match the spot of the ore.
  • If you are correct, the ore will show on your canvas, and you will be given a new ore.
  • Whoever fills their canvas back first wins!

Tips

  • If you are incorrect, you will get a four-second penalty then get a few seconds to view the original canvas again.
  • At any point when viewing the original model canvas, you may press your jump key to bring yourself to your canvas.

Pearl Hurl

Race through an ender pearl obstacle course in The End.

Rules

  • Follow the arrows on the signs and shown by blocks.
  • Your ender pearl recharges one second after throwing it.
  • First player to complete the course wins!

Tips

  • Ties are determined by the first player to complete the most recent checkpoint.

Take Flight

Elytra rocket racing.

Rules

  • Fly though yellow squares for a speed boost.
  • If you die, you lose momentum and respawn where you are at.
  • The first player to complete three laps wins!

Tips

  • There is a 1/2 second minimum cooldown between each rocket use.

Whack-A-Mob

Just like whack-a-mole but with Minecraft monsters.

Rules

  • You get points for hitting more monsters.
  • Each monster requires a full hit from the axe to earn points.
  • The game ends after 90 seconds.
  • Score the most points to win!

Tips

  • The point values for hitting each monster is as follows:
  • Zombies: 1 Point
  • Zombified Piglin: 2 Points
  • Zombies with Armor and Wither Skeletons: 3 Points
  • Endermen: 5 Points
  • Blazes: -1 Point
  • Zombies with armor take two hits to kill.
  • Wither skeletons last for shorter time.
  • Enderman will disappear and reappear in the same position.

One Shot Wonder

You get one arrow that instantly kills. Rack up the most kills to win.

Rules

  • Arrows are always insta-kills on players.
  • If you miss your arrow shot, you must kill using your sword.
  • The player with the most kills at the end of the game wins!

Tips

  • You receive your arrow back if you kill someone or die.
  • You respawn after a few seconds.
  • The game ends after 90 seconds.

Odd One Out

One villager looks different. Find it quickly.

Rules

  • When the round begins, you must find and right-click the villager that looks different.
  • If you click an incorrect villager, you will receive slowness for a short period of time.
  • The player with the most points at the end wins!

Tips

  • If you find the villager first, you will receive more points compared to everyone else.
  • If you fail to find the villager in time, you earn 0 points.
  • The game ends after 3 rounds.

Sheep Scramble

Guess the number of sheep in a herd.

Rules

  • You will have 10 seconds to view the entire herd.
  • When the time is up, you must guess the number of sheep in the herd by triggering an objective in the chat.
  • Whoever guesses closest to the actual number of sheep wins!

Trident Tumblers

Rules

  • Use your riptide II trident to launch yourself up the platforms.
  • The first player to get to the top platform wins.

Puzzled

Rules

  • The image will be scrambled and randomly rotated on the left side.
  • Match the left side to the image on the right side to win!

Boss Battle

All players fight the same boss at once. Deal as much damage as possible to the boss.

Rules

  • The boss you will face is the Ender Mutant.
  • The Ender Mutant spawns endermite minions and shoots dragon breath once in a while.
  • Deal the most overall damage to the boss to win!

Tips

  • The Ender Mutant will teleport you to one of its endermite minions when it hits you.
  • Be careful as you lose half of your points for dying.
  • The game ends after the boss gets killed.

Minecraft Party Intelligence Quiz

Rules
