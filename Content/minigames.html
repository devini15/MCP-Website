Minigames

Free For All

Dropper

Fall down through randomly generated rings from the world height limit to the bottom of the world.

Rules

  • Weave your way through the rings. You cannot go around the rings.
  • To win the game, you must be the first player to reach the bottom.

Tips

  • If you die, you start back up at the top.
  • For players that do not reach the bottom, the winner is chosen between whoever makes it the farthest.

Monster Shoot

Shoot monsters falling from the sky. Each monster is worth different points.

Rules

  • The point values are as follows:
  • Zombie: 1 Point
  • Creeper: 2 Points
  • Skeleton: 3 Points
  • Silverfish: 5 Points

Tips

  • The game ends after 60 seconds. Earn the most points to win!

Cave Parkour

Parkour through 15 checkpoints in an abandoned cave with a randomly chosen path.

Rules

  • Step over redstone lamps to reach checkpoints.
  • Reach the end of the course first to win or have the most checkpoints completed when the game ends.

Tips

  • There are 15 total checkpoints. Drop flag to reset to checkpoint.

Raft Racers

Complete two through an ice and water course on a raft. Six different paths possible.

Rules

  • You will go through different ice curves, launch pads, bubble columns, and water.

Tips

  • Drop flag to bring your boat to you if your boat gets stuck.
  • First person to finish 2 laps wins!

Knockout

Punch players out of the map with a knockback stick.

Rules

  • Knock each other off of the platforms.
  • Each player has 3 lives. Last player alive wins!

Tips

  • Knocking a player off earns you a knockback 2 stick.
