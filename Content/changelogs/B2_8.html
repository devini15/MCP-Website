Minecraft Party Beta 2.8


Additions

  • Fresh new build for Betris arena

Changes

  • Pac-Maze is no longer visible from the Slime Time arena
  • Updated Sigils Video link
  • Speed Builders now announces when players finish
  • Fight! Fight! Fight! now grants 1 level of projectile protection to each player
  • Reduced number of Arrows granted in Fight! Fight! Fight! kit (5× → 4×)
  • Item icons now display above the chests in Port Priority
  • Meat Slap now prints the Ticks Tagged score (used to break ties) to chat at end of game
  • Players can now leave adventure mode without breaking the bonus star ceremony
  • Reduced duration of Bat Blast (60s → 45s)
  • Changed star cost announcement wording on Lost City
  • Rearranged quick options in hub
  • Modified facing direction of post-swap Minion Pet

Fixes

  • Port Town would sometimes fail to summon a star seller
  • Lost City would sometimes fail to summon a star seller
  • Candyland would sometimes fail to summon a star seller
  • Port Town Whirlpool did not behave correctly
  • Players could fall through the barriers above Port Town
  • Players could lose hunger in Tunnel o' Fun
  • Port Town did not teleport non-players to the Bew room
  • Random gravel stack appeared in Hide & Seek arena
  • Under extremely specific circumstances, the bonus star ceremony would get skipped
  • Players can score 118 points in Rocket Launch without deploying their elytra
  • Tunnel o' Fun generation reworked to prevent a bug where the last cylinder would fail to load
  • Minion Pet would softlock the game if swapped while between spaces
  • Thrice and Twice dice would multiply Mini Dice when activated on the same turn
  • Extra Chance Time spaces appear on Lost City when scrambling spaces
  • Players were able to receive a Minion Pet, Coin Snatcher, or Crazy Clock, even if they already had max items
  • Market Mayhem sign is visible from Pig Pushers arena
  • Players were able to lag through some of the walls in Sky Fly
  • Swap colors event would briefly teleport players to Candyland when activated on Lost City
