Minecraft Party 2.5


Additions

  • Added new versions of 1v3s for various different player counts including: 1v2s, 2v3s, 2v4s, 2v5s
  • NEW MINIGAME: Hog Riders
  • Grand Prix mode compatibility for odd player counts above 4
  • Language parameterization
  • New battle minigame music during a special event
  • Some MPIQ questions that were harder to recall are now "personalized"
  • More accessible keybind options for Boom Carts as well as controls on sidebar
  • Messages in chat for when a player approaches a single-use star seller on Lost City or Candyland
  • Hub Translations for hats and Master Settings

Changes

  • Added barriers blocking off player until player put music disc in the jukebox after beating the music disc challenge on Zen Garden
  • Slowed down movement of pawns in Balance Beam
  • Changed the knockback stick text color in gold for gold players and aqua for aqua players (if applicable)
  • Honey Boost item is no longer consumable
  • Sign pointing to ice plunge challenge on Present Palace
  • Passing the side of the block in Balance Beam now counts as a checkpoint
  • Raft Racers now only uses the end course
  • Bew approach text now appears for all players on Mooshroom Grotto
  • Translation keys for various signs across the map
  • Changed how Tanks works so that the players control skeletons and the roles are unique
  • Improved villager interaction detection for Odd One Out
  • 4th Place no longer shows up in Stats Book for Mini-party Mode
  • Changed star seller block clone coordinates on Port Town so that the star seller blocks are hidden
  • Fireworks now spawn higher for single use star seller spawning event so that the fireworks do not spawn and explode inside of a barrier
  • Interaction added on Bew's ship to prevent trapdoor flicking on PT
  • Pearls in Pearl Hurl now glow in the color of the team of the thrower
  • Changed large arena glass color in Pac Cube to be more distinct compared to aqua team's outlines
  • Egg reloads happen automatically in Mouse Trap with a max of 2 instead of 1
  • Light added to Fort Suits victory podiums
  • Reactor Reactor micro duel now uses spooky music
  • More light added to Boat Race course for checkpoint detection in case a player lags past
  • Players receive night vision during Bombs Away!
