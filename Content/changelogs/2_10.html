Minecraft Party 2.10


Additions

  • The number of spaces a player has to choose from is now included in chat when the player uses their ender warp ability
  • Added 'Capitalism' and 'Tax Collection' green space events
  • Controls added to sidebar in Cannoneers
  • Allowed for right click detection for various items in the following minigames: Cave Parkour, Raft Racers, Tile Capture, Horse Race

Changes

  • Reroll tokens now get cleared after a player buys a star
  • Heart fountain sacrifices buffed from 4 Coins/Karma to 5 Coins/Karma on Fort Suits
  • Ender Guardian pass space message now shows for all players on Lost City
  • Best of 3 Micro Duels (Grand Prix Mode) now alternates between duel player roles
  • Players now receive weakness before Team Hockey and Turtle Hockey

  • Copper ingot in diamonds quadrant challenge did not get cleared after a player passed the next space on FS

  • Cuckoo Clock hover text description was incorrect in Turn Mode
  • Item would not get killed in Tile Capture when dropped by the dark blue player
  • Diagonal movement vector in 'What The Cluck?' is now normalized to prevent players from moving faster than they should
  • Dusty Dunes temple generation would not work if chunks were not loaded
  • Premium Reroll Token auto-chose the roll for the player if the player was in spectator mode when the roll options were given
  • Item names were not displaying on the signs when the surprise shops setting was turned off
  • Fence at 134 63 -5152 was connected to food barrel on Suburbia Scramble
  • Players can trigger spectate to get on the board in adventure mode when it is not their turn on SS
  • Players were able to hit other players during the minigame cutscene if the following conditions were true: board was SS, previous turn included a player attempting the picnic panic challenge, previous turn included a minigame after the picnic challenge (from landing on a space)
  • Double star voucher and reroll tokens were unable to get enhanced nor duplicated in Vulcan's room on FS
  • 'Random player' option for Mini Dice could activate on a spectator
  • Players could respawn in the hub when playing Recruitment Royale if they die on the last tick of the recruitment phase
