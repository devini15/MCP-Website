Minecraft Party Beta 2.83


Additions

  • New World Border in What the Cluck?
  • Player Pawns now hold a shield in their offhand when the Spirit Shield(&+) is active

Changes

  • Spirit Shields now protect players from Lightning and Anvil Traps
  • Tellraw of coin payouts now put "Coins" in gold
  • MPIQ now has hosts
  • Updated the Sandy/Candy Cannon design
  • Players are now reminded to reset the old player score, and updating old joiners to match their teammate's score when swapping out players
  • Shops are now opened on the last turn in Free for All games if the Shopping Star is enabled
  • In large games (6+ players) orbs can no longer can spawn in the exact middle of the arena in Maze Navigator
  • Players can now escape the end game room
  • The pass spot message when approaching the slot machine now shows to all players on Center Park
  • Invisible armor stands are now markers in Cat Catchers
  • Merchant messages are no longer bold on CP

  • The emerald block appear thing looked bad in Just Joust
  • If a player with no coins landed on the same space as another player during a special event with micro duels and 10 coin wager as the same space duel setting and there were no other micro duels for the remaining turn, a micro duel would run at the end of the last player's turn (rather than the regular end of turn minigame) with no participants
  • Players could see the new board from Rhythmic Room
  • Board specific stars could get awarded twice
  • Platform colors did not get set correctly in Duel minigame cutscenes
  • There was weird spot detection issues when going back to the starting space on Arcane Atoll
  • The star fragment icon above a player pawn would not disappear if the player lost the star fragment due to a different player who did have a star fragment stealing from them
  • Wrong value was used for "(Increased Rolls: +3)" in the language translation
  • Racing and Elimination minigames couldn't be played in Quickplay with 1 player
  • Pairs mode prefixes weren't translated
  • Tickets wouldn't display on the sidebar when dropped (pairs/doubles) on CP
  • Stealable items did not display in the actionbar for Player Swiper
  • Hitting the bottom of the flying treasure had an invulnerable entity on Port Town
  • TNT Launching was messy near the free item pickup on PT
  • 1v3s could get rolled in doubles mode when only 6 players were present
  • "Freeze in Place" text display showed up on the space one before the Freeze Trap on PT
  • The Candyland Path Challenge and the lava path in temples on Dusty Dunes could generate weird paths
  • The skeleton would randomly die from nothing happening after about 20 seconds of the Flying Treasure challenge on PT
  • The message for disabling Bew's ship did not get translated on PT
  • Two armor pieces could spawn in the same location on CP
  • Ticket shop entering points could spawn Star Sellers/Coin Piles/Equipment on CP
  • "Marker" would show up in text in the stats book
  • Sometimes arrows on the intersections would not get updated to their correct state after swapping with Minion/Twinion Pets
