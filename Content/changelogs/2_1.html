Minecraft Party 2.1


Additions

  • NEW BOARD: Fort Suits
  • New 2v2 minigame music (including end of game version)
  • New hub music
  • Old hub music now moved to Grand Prix Mode
  • Old 2v2 end of game music now moved to Duels (end of game)
  • Added wind to Balance Beam to indicate the direction of pull
  • Changed "Clockwise" and "Counterclockwise" in Betris controls on sidebar to symbols (to take up less space)

Changes

  • Armor stands smaller in Platforms so they don't block players from clicking signs
  • Increased height of some trees in Boss Battle so that the Boss can fit under them
  • Diamond boots in Gold Rush now give temporary immunity from slime rather than one time immunity
  • The points score for Aqua team is now underlined
  • More light in barn in Sheep Scramble
  • Players could get stuck in a section of the end game room
  • Got rid of speed boost from boosted players in Take Flight upon death
  • Receiving a star from the sacrificial altar now plays the star collection sound
  • Got rid of "Which minigame was played during turn _?" if the minigame cycle restarts for a free for all/2v2/1v3 in MPIQ
  • Reduced strength of gravity pull in Balance Beam
  • Modified equipment strength for all sets (except Speedy set) so that the chestplate is slightly stronger and the boots are slightly weaker
  • Reworded Star Protector (&+) descriptions
  • Ender equipment now has shorter cooldown for initial first use and differing decrease in current active cooldown (depending on the piece obtained)
  • Rebalanced In The Zone (2v6 version) in favor of the team of 2
  • Market Mayhem now stays at night if played during the night
  • Tellraw added in Path Finder when the minigame finishes that says in chat: "[Player name] completed the path!"
  • Rebalanced 2v6 Mouse Trap: 55 seconds -> 52 seconds

Fixes

  • Random issues with team colors not updating when swapping out players
  • Collision was on for players queued up as spectators in the hub
  • Players would not receive coins in Mini-Party mode when sitting out of a minigame
  • Maximum value was set incorrectly in the bossbar for Boss Battle
  • Some players' trails would not disappear after getting eliminated in Color Trails
  • Players unable to buy star after using a Warp To Star (&+) on Candyland
  • On rare instances (chunk loading issue), certain board entities (such as traps) would not get correctly cleared from the previous game
  • Two of the text displays in the Customize minigames room has the wrong text
  • Sometimes items would not get synced up in pairs/teams mode if the 'Swap Items' event occurred in chance time
  • Sidebar would randomly go blank in 2v6 Pac Cube
  • Missing space after " and" in 2v6 Ravager Rodeo rules
  • Missing bed in Market Mayhem
  • Spirit Shield activator item lore in wrong text color
