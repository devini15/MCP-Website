Minecraft Party Beta 2.11


Additions

  • Honey Boost now appears in hub item room
  • Mega Teams mode
  • NEW MINIGAME: Boom Carts (2v2)
  • Stats book now has a page for the board-specific bonus stat

Changes

  • Candyland path challenge now awards more coins for more complex paths
  • If a player passes another player while they and the other player both have Coin Thieves active, the player being passed will activate first
  • Center Park shops now have more items (4 → 6)
  • Center Park shops now close once used
  • Increased delay between rocket give commands (500ms → 2s)

Fixes

  • Lost City sometimes allows first space of a turn to be skipped if the player moves too quickly
  • PvP was enabled in Bat Blast
  • Players who were previously stuck in the floor in Dragon Doom became stuck in the wall
  • Tying for 1st in a 4fa would not award double coins during Double Trouble
  • Players could get stuck at a certain spot on Candyland
  • Players get banished to outer end if they enter the portal too early in Pearl Hurl
  • Chance Time item swap did not include Honey Boost
  • Chump Charity would go to 4th place even if there were more than 4 players
  • Knockout placements were scuffed
  • Double Star Voucher scoreboard not present in Candyland
  • Some of the armor stands in the Candyland victory room are floating (spooky)
  • Stimulus only distributed coins to players 1-4, even in MPM
  • Players were able to exchange keys in Labyrinth
  • Players would get sent to oblivion instead of the spectation area when eliminated from Mine Your Business
  • Too many barriers in the Star Ceremony area
  • Players are given back their items after using a reroll token
  • Hardini's challenge broke one of the functions used for some cutscenes and minigames
  • Devin's RNG was skewed towards bad outcomes
