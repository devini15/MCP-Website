Minecraft Party Beta 2.74


Additions

  • Enhanced Items now have obfuscated characters by their names to make them way cooler
  • Various minigame arenas got revamped
  • Subtitle text now tells players how to pick up the flag in Capture the Flag

Changes

  • Minion/Twinion pets can no longer receive a Minion/Twinion pet if they land on a Yellow Space
  • Receiving a star from a Star Counterfeiter is much quieter
  • Updated Pirate Language translations
  • Controls were changed to be easier to understand in Cannoneers
  • Barriers are now waterlogged in Go Fish!
  • The ceiling now damages players in Laser Evaders
  • Victory rooms are much bigger on Arcane Atoll

Fixes

  • Triple Star Discount Voucher description would update when switching to the enhanced items display in the item room
  • Reroll and Premium reroll tokens would break during the Increased Rolls event on AA
  • Zen Garden was very mean to people
  • Equipment would spawn on the new shops on ZG
  • TNT would rarely spawn before the minigame started in Bombs Away!
  • Players could still receive the "Teleport to Turn Player" item even after commands cleared it from their inventory
  • The team of 3 could get on top of the trees using a fallen birch log in Treetop Hop
  • Some spectators wouldn't get teleported to Roll Off/Chance Time on Pipe Canyon
  • Magenta Space display text had inconsistent capitalization on PC.
  • Title times were not set properly for rolling a Magic Dice
  • Down elevators wouldn't work on the last turn on Mooshroom Grotto
  • Moved player line to be on the stone slabs on PC
  • Juice sign still said "Wine" on Lofty Luncheon
  • Equipment would close when rerolling on the space right before it
  • Using Bew now prevents swapping with a minion until passing the next space and also clears the turn player's reroll tokens(&+)
  • Players were unable to access the roof of the house on Block Busters
  • Shops sometimes wouldn't open with the surprise shops setting turned off
  • Players would get stuck when flying inside the pipe in the lobby
