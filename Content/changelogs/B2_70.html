Minecraft Party Beta 2.70


Additions

  • Settings for a game are displayed in the stats book
  • Players are alerted of who cracked the code in Decryption
  • New Special Event: All items become enhanced

  • Chance time space near the picnic challenge on Suburbia Scramble is now a blue space
  • Players move back and forth automatically in Just Joust
  • Answer choices have a greater difference between each one for some questions in MPIQ
  • MPIQ is more likely to appear as a minigame only in a special event
  • Minigame category signs adjust in quickplay according to the player count
  • Text arrows appear on both sides of signs on Lost City
  • Port Town Bonus Star renamed (Coin Stealer Star -> Coin Thief Star)
  • Aquor has more detection range for the dripleaf challenge on Arcane Atoll
  • Speed Armor roll bonus text is cyan
  • Coin Thief Star on PT tells players that traps and cannons count towards the star
  • Border in Treetop Hop has been raised
  • Minigame priority defaults to include all minigame types
  • Reduced time limit in Ravager Rodeo (90s -> 80s)
