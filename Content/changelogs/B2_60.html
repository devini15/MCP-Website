  • Ghost Mode invisibility toggling broke when players left the board mid-turn
  • Micro duel activation radius was too large on PT

  • Hotbar config was incorrect for Capture the Flag

  • PM did not allow double-dipping free items using warps
  • PM had incorrect barrier placements after one of the warps
  • PM shops did not open when warped near
