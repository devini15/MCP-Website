Minecraft Party Beta 2.66


Additions

  • Realms Mode support for turn skipping
  • Realms Mode support for game cancellation
  • Realms Mode support for minigame resets
  • Equipment spawn rate tuning in settings
  • More playtesters in audience

Changes

  • Arrows in minigames where invisibility is a mechanic are now piercing
  • Modified equipment defense points
  • Players can now exit Mooshroom Grotto cauldron
  • Recovered items now appear in inventory
  • Game now checks turn player is online every tick while on board
  • Updated Superstar Armor wording when buying a star
  • Superstar Armor now only awards 1 Star Shard per armor piece
  • Renamed "Custom" bonus star to "Board Specific" star
  • Modified some equipment armor trims
  • Speed Builders pickaxes are now gold instead of netherite
  • Players now get Jump Boost III in Speed Builders
  • Minions now have a brief cooldown on being converted in Minions
  • Separated Don't Push My Buttons title text into title and subtitle
  • Players are no longer able to adjust cannon power after locking shot in Cannoneers

Fixes

  • All boards had ★★★★★ complexity in hub
  • Settings room was in absolute shambles
  • Spot detection was broken on one MG space
  • "Two Star Shards" hover event was inaccurate on MG
  • Misplaced barriers on part of MG
  • Special Event stage signs did not clear after certain events
  • Recovering a Premium Reroll token would grant the player an additional Reroll Token as a treat
  • Multiple red mushrooms could appear in Pipe Canyon warp maze
  • Under certain conditions, Player Swapper would break the food arrows on Lofty Luncheon
  • White spaces could appear too close to shops
