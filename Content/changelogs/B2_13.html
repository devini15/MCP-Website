Minecraft Party Beta 2.13


Additions

  • NEW GAMEMODE: Pairs (8p)

Changes

  • Players will now be killed if they try to cheese Hole in the Wall by riding the Walls
  • Memory lane now sends each player their own room instead of congregating them in a waiting room
  • It is no longer possible to stand atop the buttons in Labyrinth
  • Ghost Hunt now has twice as many villagers in 2v6
  • Players no longer get teleported inside each other in the victory room
  • Players now spawn across from their teammate in Tile capture, spleef, gladiators, and Tedious Tasks

Fixes

  • Some decorative blocks did not appear in Betris
  • BIG HOLE in the floor would appear in Memory Lane
  • Players 5-10 did not receive coins from an opponent landing on a black space
  • Placement teleports were incorrect for 2v6 minigames
  • Hardini tried to send spectating players to space when he is ignored
  • Spleef placements were incorrect in Mega Party
  • Players could lose their emeralds in Market Mayhem by using Ctrl + Q
  • Guardian boss health bars did not display to players 5-10
  • Not all players scores were broadcast in chat after Boss Battle
  • Boss Brawl boss team were able to move before the game started in 2v6
  • Players would be suffocated in the barriers surrounding Hole in the Wall
  • Multiple server Operators simultaneously clicking separate options from the "void minigame" menu broke things very badly
  • Lost City victory room spawned some of the audience inside of blocks
  • Floating Islands allowed an invalid star spawn to be active
  • Devin's RNG was skewed towards bad outcomes
