Minecraft Party Beta 2.42


Additions

  • Decorative headlights on vehicles on SS
  • Honey Boost/Zoom now have a rare chance of being granted by orange spaces
  • Arrow pointing to Picnic Panic in SS
  • Minecraft Party 2 Music slider in audio settings
  • Custom biomes on various boards

Changes

  • Solidifying in Block Buster is now done by standing still instead of dropping an item
  • Cl chocolate river is now liquid
  • Cl sky is now magenta tinted
  • Redecorated LC
  • Modified PT water color
  • Cauldrons in MG now have colored fluids
  • Fog in Quick Draw is now tinted orange
  • Adjusted "last 5 turns" music timings for some boards to loop cleaner
  • Mouse Trap rules now tell the player they can jump to go faster
  • Updated MG elevator animation
  • Turn Stealer value is now double that of Turn Skipper in MG altars
  • Star Shards are now capitalized properly when converted
  • Rewrote Path Finder rules for clarity
  • Updated sound channel info in Dragon Doom rules
  • Port Priority beacon beams are now black to improve contrast against the skybox
  • Added extra blocks to some Hole in the Wall walls to better hint where the player needs to stand

Fixes

  • Approaching the hub from below as a spectator would send you plummeting
  • Typo in What the Cluck? rules
  • What the Cluck? cauldron could be entered
  • Players with high ping would appear to fall through the platforms in Platforms on their client
  • Typo in Freeze Trap description
  • Port Priority took its sweet time to teleport spectators
  • Having a Minion Pet land on a magenta space on SS would update the nearest restaurant to the player instead of the pet
  • Players would sometimes be able to wander around client-side after landing in Rocket Launch
  • Some blocks on MG were out of place near the spider challenge
  • Not all relevant duel tags would be cleared after a duel
  • Players could get stuck on the 1st MG magenta space due to barriers not clearing
  • MG altars gave Player Swipers with incorrect item descriptions
  • Hide & Seek arena needed roof repairs
  • 1v3 cutscenes would display incorrectly for groups of 5-7 players
  • In the Zone (1v3) would put players in the wrong starting positions in lobbies with over 4 players
  • Player Swiper updated action bar for non-turn players
  • Ghost Hunt still had outdated information in the rules
  • Mine Your Business did not handle odd player counts correctly
  • Market Mayhem would place non-players on the scoreboard if they were in adventure mode
  • 1v3 minigames would not start correctly from quickplay with 5-7 players in lobby
