Minecraft Party Beta 2.20


Additions

  • Angry villager particles when hit in Boom Carts
  • Icon showing if a player has a spirit shield appears on Bew menu

Changes

  • Hub music now plays over music disc output channel
  • Nerfed knockback level for team of 2 in 2v6 In the Zone (IV → III)
  • Pac-Maze warps now use hotbar-based menus instead of chat-based menus
  • Lone players now receive haste in Mouse Trap
  • Arrows now disappear once they have landed in Boss Brawl
  • Increased duration of 2v6 Mouse Trap (40s → 55s)
  • Tunnel o' Fun rules are now resource-pack friendly
  • 1v3s no longer playable with 5, 6, or 7 players in quickplay
  • Stocking Up advancement now requires using 4 items in 1 turn to be collected

Fixes

  • Pairs scoreboard would not render correctly on game start
  • Sidebar would not display during a Duel menu, Chance Time, or Roll Off
  • Pac-Maze shops did not open for players who have taken warps
  • In the Zone rules did not reflect Knockback level reduction
  • Pac-Maze power-pellets allowed players to send their own teammates back to start
  • Some of the Pac-Maze walls did not change color after the special event
  • Chat message for getting a Mini Dice from a free item pickup placed the hover event on the wrong text
  • Players were able to place traps on Power Pellet spawns (non-spaces) in Pac-Maze
  • Stats book rendered incorrectly after pairs/doubles mode
  • TNT would explode instantly when thrown in Bombs Away!
  • Under certain conditions, the game would erroneously report a draw for the Gold Star.
  • Some players prefixes did not display on tab list
  • Some players did not receive credit for the final checkpoint in Pearl Hurl
  • Players did not receive fire resistance after drinking the potion on Floating Islands
  • Extra word in one of the FI quest tellraws
  • Players did not receive strength after drinking the potion on Floating Islands
  • Players retain their inventories from the board at game end
  • Playing Star Race mode would break movement detection in subsequent games
  • Extraneous barrier was above board on Mooshroom Grotto
  • First junction on MG was impassible
  • Honey Boost bonus movement would not stack if multiple were used in one turn
  • Some functions were still not compliant with new command syntax
  • Canceling traps would kill several necessary board entities
  • Error with one of the Zen Garden star spawns
  • Shop messages would appear at inappropriate times on Zen Garden
  • Scrambling board spaces on ZG would result in too many Blue spaces
  • 3rd place minigame tellraw was inaccurate if there was a tie for 1st
  • Short grass did not get killed when dropped in Block Buster
  • ZG trident challenge teleports out of bounds players incorrectly
  • Dumb stupid sign that's wrong about everything in Hide & Seek attic
  • Devin's RNG was skewed towards bad outcomes
