Minecraft Party Beta 2.84


Additions

  • Accommodations for < 4 players in Quickplay Mode
  • Sign in the main lobby that when clicked, returns players back to quickplay lobby
  • Option to toggle spectator/player in Quickplay Mode
  • Presents for each player now show up in sidebar on Present Palace
  • Coin payouts are now on the sidebar for battle minigames
  • Betris controls now show in sidebar
  • Star Seller now flickers when a plyer holds an item such as a Warp to Star or Star Schmoover to indicate which Star Seller would be moved or warped to
  • The main sidebar title now has different gamemode names depending on the number of players and gamemode selected
  • New textures for the Candy/Daytime Sandy and Nighttime Sandy Cannon

Changes

  • Red Light, Green Light now shows checkmark on sidebar (in order) when players finish
  • Changed "lost" color to gray and "coins" to gold for the Zen Garden shuffle event
  • Beds in Odd One Out are now block displays to prevent players from sleeping in them
  • Points in Odd One Out at the end of the minigame now display in the order of who had the most
  • Spectators get teleported to the turn player's armor stand as the final teleport after ending a cutscene
  • The floor is brighter in Maze Navigator
  • Pellets in Pac-Maze are now item displays
  • Opponents losing coins from a yellow space event is now less likely with larger player groups
  • Thrown ender pearls now trigger trap placement (Ender Trap (&+))
  • Teleported single-use star sellers to center of world rather than the void after being bought
  • More cocoa beans in barn in Farming Frenzy
  • Underlined various points scores from tellraws at the end of various minigames
  • Dusty Dunes cacti on the board diorama now have the cactus flower instead of amethyst
  • Entities in the hub now stay when quickplay mode is active (and still get killed when a minigame starts to reduce lag)

Fixes

  • Color of fireworks when a point ends in Deuce did not match player color
  • Random missing snow (sugar) on Candyland
  • Tunnel o' Fun did not generate the first time
  • The word "coins" was not in gold when displaying coins won from a coin minigame
  • Automatic star trade could occur on boards
  • 'Duel all coins' sign was not translated
  • Coins could be dropped in Tunnel o' Fun
  • Players that died in Pac Cube (and are in spectator mode) would still retain their glowing armor stand
  • Suburbia Scramble diorama containers were not locked
  • 2nd Place in Hole In The Wall could fall into the void and hit the ground hard after teleporting to the victory area
  • Cats lost their coins in Cat Catchers
  • Lost City shows coin and star amounts in containers prior to opening (though they had no effect on actual coins/stars acquired from them)
  • In rare instances, the Star Seller would be purchased after being approached
  • Items could linger after exiting the barrel/chest shop on LC and then cause lag
  • Players could lose the void minigame item in Cannoneers
  • There was a possible equipment spot near Miner on Mooshroom Grotto that was impossible to trigger
  • A double trouble minigame (double coin rewards) would occur twice when the last player triggered the event
  • Packed ice would not replace the light blocks when a player hit a freeze trap on LC
  • Duel signs were still showing the word "Duel" followed by the player name in the old player colors
  • Untranslated PM warp selectors
  • Various issues with team and role selection in the hub when quickplay was active
  • Power Pellet sound did not get canceled when a player used a Reroll Token
  • Ravager Rodeo items did not get killed
  • Ravager could get stuck if it hit the wall in a specific orientation in Ravager Rodeo
  • Equipment would not get spawned on PM
  • Ender Aura traps would not get spawned when returning to the board
  • There were issues with setting the flag to refresh the spot arrows and highlighted spaces relating to offset arrows
  • When using a Warp To Star followed by a Gold Bar, players' coins would not get updated
  • Big Magic Dice Player selector heads would not get cleared properly which caused the Plus and Minus roll changer banners to end up in the incorrect hotbar slots
