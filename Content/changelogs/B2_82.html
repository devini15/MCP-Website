Minecraft Party Beta 2.82


Changes

  • Players can now swap with themselves with the Player Swapper/Turn Stealer
  • A new arrow now makes the path more clear at the end of Raft Racers
  • Numerous messages and sounds now output to all players.
  • Players can no longer open tolls if they have landed

  • The turn player would lose coins with a Thorned Spirit Shield active after passing a player with a Pocket Plunderer
  • Barriers would block players and players wern't getting TP'd to their spawning locations in Snow Wars
  • Incorrect number of scrambled spaces of certain colors across various boards
  • Item file in resource pack was not up-to-date in Ravager Rodeo
  • White spot detection issue at a shop on CL
  • Coin Snatcher/Stealer text was the wrong color when receiving it
  • P1's name was not on the scoreboard in the turn order section in the star ceremony
  • If the last player in the turn order used a Turn Stealer, at the end of the stolen turn, that player would stay in adventure mode after the turn ended and not receive the item that puts them into spectator mode when dropped if this player was previously spectating when it wasn't their turn
  • Items that Chester gives/takes were not in bold
  • Equipment on certain spaces was enabled in CL immediately after gates
  • The Never Ending Game advancement was not achievable
