Minecraft Party Beta 2.78


Additions

  • Most Board specific Bonus Stars now have the ability to be enabled on any board
  • New Bonus Star: Explorer Star
  • Translated Equipment Lore

Changes

  • Base prices for Ghost Mode(&+) are different for various boards
  • Altered dynamic pricing so that the average price for each player should be equal to the base price as opposed to being slightly lower
  • A message in chat now indicates which team finished in Prison Pals
  • A keybind/trigger objective now shows the void minigame message in chat for players
  • "Coin Thief" Star is now in Pirate Speak

Fixes

  • Players could repeatedly go on the first space on the right, right after descending the spiral staircase on Arcane Atoll
  • Players would get stuck in the Mob holes if jumped into immediately after spawning in Recruitment Royale
  • Spell fireworks had barriers that caused them to collide on AA
  • Spectators would get TP'd when only a specific team was supposed to be TP'd
  • Port Priority and Puzzled did not end with the 'fixed amount for winning' setting enabled
  • Incorrect Pirate translations
