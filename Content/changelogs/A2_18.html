Minecraft Party Alpha 2.18


Additions

  • The datapack now cares about your feelings
  • Death message when you run out of stock in Knockout
  • Wall to block off incorrect path in Horse Race
  • Message for when players complete the course in Take Flight

Changes

  • Fire Trap now stacks with Double Trouble

  • Implemented better lighting at night in Block Buster
  • Changed some colors on Floating Islands
  • Lowered death floor in Bombs Away!
  • Modified Hide and Seek arena
  • Ghost Hunt villagers are now removed when the game ends
  • Draw pedestals for duels

Fixes

  • Spectators on scoreboard in Steve Says
  • Spectators on scoreboard in Whack-a-Mob
  • Spectators on scoreboard in Monster Shoot
  • Playing Memory Lane in practice mode would brick the game
  • Memory Lane would delete your emergency slime ball
  • Center Park would sometimes fail to summon necessary entities
  • Center Park would sometimes teleport players to the wrong spots
  • Tunnel o' Fun was vertically challenged
  • Center Park did not show arrows at an intersection after the player warped there
  • Game sometimes got confused as to which trap you have when displaying your inventory in chat
  • Fire Trap did not activate if you started your turn on it
  • Spectators not teleported to Knockout properly
  • Spectators missed some Steve Says orders in chat
  • Finish line did not count as a checkpoint in Horse Race
