Minecraft Party Alpha 2.5


Additions

  • Sign text on Floating Islands
  • Quest items now have lore text

Changes

  • Changed board description on Floating Islands
  • Moved signs 1 block higher in platforms
  • Items in containers are now removed between turns
  • Solidified players are now in spectator mode in BlockBuster
  • Changed text in some quests on Floating Islands
  • Players now heal after Mine and Kill ends
  • Chunks are now preemptively loaded in Take Flight
  • Increased Knockback to 4 in In the Zone
  • Rebalanced Quest rewards
  • Star sellers can no longer spawn on certain spaces
  • Reduced the number of words in the names of some minigames
  • Changed ship builds to be copyright safe in Port Priority
  • There is no longer a countdown when you respawn in Pac Cube
  • Modified checkpoint after library in Cave Parkour
  • Nerfed power-up duration in Pac Cube
  • Optimized tick function for Floating Islands
  • Coin Thief is now a passive ability that steals 12 coins from the next player you pass (will steal from multiple players if passed on the same turn)
  • The lone player now spawns further away from the platform in In the Zone

Fixes

  • Some items took your dice away
  • Fence post moved incorrectly on Floating Islands
  • Minidice was described too excitedly
  • Some spaces were not traversable on Floating Islands
  • Some arrows were missing on Floating Islands
  • Certain items cleared your inventory, even when they shouldn't've
  • Some Quest dialogue would display incorrectly
  • The first trap used would not work
  • Players would spawn in the wrong dimension for Take Flight
  • Sometimes, players would not get the 1st key in Port Priority
  • No campfire in Block Buster
  • Some players were unable to view the board description
  • Minigames got mildly fucky if too many people were online
  • Chance time sometimes softlocked the game on Floating Islands
  • Destroyed crops persisted between games in Market Mayhem
  • Runner was a bit too eager to kill you
  • Poor people could teleport
  • Issue with minigames when players are swapped out
  • You could become a dandelion or grass in an invalid state in Block Buster
  • Quest items did not leave your inventory when another player completed the relevant quest on Floating Islands
