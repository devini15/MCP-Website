Minecraft Party Alpha 2.20


Additions

  • Arrow to door unlock lever in Prison Pals
  • Players get Dolphin's Grace in Port Priority
  • Gold Rush instructions say you can mine gold after time runs out
  • Identifying signs on teleporters on Floating Islands
  • More playtesters added to armor stand collection
  • New board event to Port Town
  • Randomize Spaces is now an option in settings

Changes

  • Moved Cobwebs from Boss Brawl to be less game-ending
  • Sacrificial Altar no longer loud as hell
  • Slimes are now slightly slower in Slime Time
  • Player facing direction now matches pawn facing direction at start of turn (should be facing the right way)
  • Cave Parkour checkpoints are now redstone lamps instead of purpur
  • Hiders now have slowness in Block Buster
  • Cave spaces now visible from above in Floating Islands
  • Player Scores are now updated more frequently
  • Swapping out players now updates their armor stand's name
  • Slightly modified order of events that occur after a roll

Fixes

  • Players could run to the wrong safety zone in Rock Paper Scissors Chase and still be safe
  • Hole in barriers above the board in Floating Islands
  • Players could corner cut a space on Floating Islands
  • A quest sign was missing from Floating Islands
  • Spectators were not teleported correctly after Boat Race
  • Under very specific conditions, the game would softlock after a Battle Minigame
  • Orange spaces would only give Thrice Dice
  • Star Protector had the wrong description in the Item Room
  • Star Sellers would reproduce asexually on Center Park
  • Coin piles looked weird due to barriers in the way of them spawning
  • Slot Machine chest gave out incorrect number of coins
  • One of the Tile Capture arenas did not teleport spectators properly
  • Spectators appeared on the scoreboard in Tile Capture
  • Traps did not work in Center Park
