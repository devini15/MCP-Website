Minecraft Party Beta 2.22


Additions

  • Traffic Whistle in Hub item room
  • Added blindness for the start of Battle and Coin minigame cutscenes
  • Signs for new playtesters in Hub
  • NEW BOARD: Pipe Canyon
  • 20s Time limit on Center Park Slots

Changes

  • Mr. P now calls to you from further away
  • Slime movement speed slightly increased in Slime Time
  • Updated trap placement UI to be more intuitive
  • Gold team's Fun Stick in Rock Paper Scissors Chase now has Gold display text
  • Players who join mid-game are now automatically placed in spectator mode

Fixes

  • Players were unable to leave start space on Center Park
  • Some shops on Center Park did not close after a purchase was made
  • Traffic Whistle was not listed in chat at start of player turn
  • Traffic Whistle did not clear from players' inventories when it should have
  • Center Park intersection arrows do not update after using a Player Swapper
  • Some of the Center Park merchants refuse to sell their wears
  • Some of the Center Park shops had misplaced logs
  • Canceling Tedious Tasks halfway through could cause issues in other minigames
  • Ghost Mode did not eliminate tolls
  • Players could not attempt Mr. P's fishing challenge on CP due to stray barriers
  • Pac-Maze had a misplaced barrier above one of the shops
  • Center Park intersection arrows sometimes failed to appear
  • Movement enforcement barriers in the CP park occasionally caused suffocation damage
  • Miscolored stained glass in Hole in the Wall
  • Some parts of Hole in the Wall would generate incorrectly
  • Custom bonus star score was not updated correctly when bonus star scores are refreshed
  • 1.20.4 broke slot machine item frames on Center Park
