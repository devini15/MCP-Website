Minecraft Party Beta 2.17


Additions

  • Mega Party Mode compatibility to Hungry Hungry Humans for 5, 6, 7, 8, or 10 players
  • In the Pipe from Mario Party 2 now plays in the lobby
  • New playtesters in audience
  • New "Poison Mushroom" event on Mooshroom Grotto

Changes

  • Made it harder to leave your podium in the free for all podium room
  • Increased stalactite regrowth speed in Dripstone Dance (??4)
  • Enabled automatic health regeneration in Bridge Crossing
  • Increased minigame payouts for "middle of the pack" players
  • Mitigated risk of shipwreck in Raft Racers
  • Sky Fly now tells you to fall if you get stuck on the bars
  • Tridents now knock down more dripstone per trident in Dripstone Dance
  • Finding the Easter Egg in the hub now temporarily removes your hat
  • Reduced chance of getting a Star Schmoover from the MG star altar
  • Green Space outcomes are now weighted dynamically based on total coins in play
  • Mooshroom Grotto's bonus star is now decided by coins sacrificed
  • Reduced max Fire Trap activations (∞ → 3)
  • Reduced max Ice Trap activations (∞ → 3)
  • Removed cacti from Meat Slap arena

Fixes

  • The lone player could escape the fence in Dripstone Dance
  • Players could lose items in Bridge Crossing
  • Some minigames failed to end when their timer expired
  • Players were able to beneath the barriers on Mooshroom Grotto before turn 1
  • Bombs Away! would not clear all effects when voided
  • Returning to the board would sometimes say weird things in chat
  • There was a wall on one of the paths in Horse Race
  • Some minigames would still be in the rotation even when playing with an odd player count
  • Players unable to keep their grubby little hands off of the command line to troll their friends could cause a hole in the middle of Mooshroom Grotto's barrier floors to appear in some cases
  • Selecting the Variety altar in Mooshroom Grotto would trigger a roll
  • Voiding Tunnel o' Fun did not remove slow falling
  • Spelling error in MG Cauldron Challenge instructions
  • Missing red mushroom block in MG Cauldron Challenge
  • Dragon Doom would sometimes spawn a player in the ground
  • Players were unable to start a game normally after playing Quickplay
  • Pac-Maze failed to summon entities for Player 1
  • Pac-Maze Power Pellets could not be collected
  • Using a Crazy Clock could cause same-space duels to initiate at incorrect times
  • Capture the Flag would not generate flags if players readied up too quickly
  • Using the OP turn skipper before the game started would break everything
