Minecraft Party Beta 2.9


Additions

  • Steve Says elimination messages
  • Items that modify your dice roll now display the modifier as a subtitle before it is applied
  • More playtesters in audience/credits
  • Chance Time "Give All Coins" event
  • Chance Time "Give Random Item" event
  • Failsafe to postpone placements until all players are online.
  • NEW MINIGAME: Space Jumpers (2v2)

Changes

  • It is no longer possible to enter the parkour room in Prison Pals without moving your spawnpoint into the parkour room
  • Changed the team color used for all players in Creeper Catchers to white (cat teams are still color-coded)
  • Reworded Fight! Fight! Fight! death message
  • Players can no longer go through the barn to grief their opponent in Pig Pushers
  • Port Town stealing star stat now counts coins stolen via Lightning Trap
  • Updated information on some hub signs.
  • Archer in Tanks is now moved into the boat should their driver be slain in battle.

Fixes

  • Players would have to choose teams before being allowed to start Quickplay in Doubles
  • One of the Pac-Maze blockades did not work
  • Power Pellets did not appear on Pac-Maze
  • Star Protectors would not count towards the items-per-turn limit
  • Landing on an Ender Trap would still trigger the space the trap was on
  • Pac-Maze does not place any Black Spaces when "Randomize Spaces" is enabled
  • Trick or Treat would sometimes end on a cliffhanger on Candyland
  • Disc challenge returned all players to adventure mode, even if they had triggered spectation before the challenge started
  • Coin Snatcher and Star Stealer allow stealing from your doubles teammate
  • Battle Minigame space had coin update issues
  • Eggcellence glass wall failed to appear in doubles
  • Game was rigged against Gold in doubles
  • Glowing effect not given back to player upon exiting Zen Garden disc challenge
  • Mini Dice sounded weird during the Big Dice special event.
  • Spectating players could ghost in Find the Button
  • Players were able to abuse bugs to escape the Sky Fly arena
  • Players were able to use pearls and enchantment bottles in Market Mayhem
  • Players were able to use their bows before Spleef Started
  • Some players wouldn't pay their ante for Battle Minigames
  • Using the TP to Turn Player item during a Zen Garden Shuffle event would softlock the game.
