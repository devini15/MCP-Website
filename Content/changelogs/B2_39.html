Minecraft Party Beta 2.39


Additions

  • More light to spaces on Floating Islands
  • NEW ITEM [S] Big Magic Dice
  • Charging and Ramming sounds in Ravager Rodeo

Changes

  • Rebalanced Battle Minigame Wagers
  • Players are now invisible with boots in Rainbow Run
  • "Double dipping" is no longer possible in Rainbow Run
  • Fireworks in team victory room now explode at a greater height on Floating Islands
  • Floating Islands now awards super items for cooked steak quest
  • Star Stealer is now not sold in shops unless at least one player has a star
  • Rearranged hub banners
  • Ravager Rodeo now uses player set keybinds instead of default controls for instructions
  • Reduced hit detection regions in Ravager Rodeo
  • Changed timing on check for exceeding the item limit when purchasing from a shop
  • Increased Ravager Rodeo hit detection regions while still leaving them smaller than they were before aforementioned reduction
  • Premium Reroll Token can no longer roll the same number as the original roll
  • Zen garden challenge now shows correct block placement on end.
  • Battle minigames and Duel spaces no longer count towards the event star
  • Removed weakness potions and gold apples from Ghost Hunt
  • Spectators in overworld now get teleported to a player automatically after Dragon Doom
  • Other minigame arenas no longer visible from Ravager Rodeo
  • Increased volume of trumpet at the beginning of the 2v2 minigame music.

Fixes

  • One of the Floating Islands star spawns would spawn and extra villager
  • Treasure Bag description sign did not work
  • Tile capture would spawn all players in the middle of the arena if certain settings were enabled
  • Orange spaces called Binary Dice "Mini Dice"
  • One of the coin pile spawns on Floating Islands was uncollectable
  • Coin Stealer item lore was inaccurate
  • Grand Prix mode could be started with an odd number of players
  • Ravager Rodeo columns were not cloned
  • Ravager Rodeo failed to end if a player was eliminated after time ran out
  • Micro Duel wagers would be calculated incorrectly in some cases
  • Zen Garden coin shuffle did not assess player eligibility correctly
  • Player Swiper would show action bar text to all players
  • Pickpocketer and Pocket Plunderer would steal coins for every space traveled when used in conjunction with a Turn Stealer
  • Freeze Trap and Mini Dice were not removed from target inventory when a Player Swiper was used
  • Trap items on the board would sometimes get displaced
  • There was a spot on Zen Garden that allowed players to go backwards
  • ZG block guessing challenge could be replayed
  • The Ravager in Ravager Rodeo would sometimes clip into a wall and get stuck.
