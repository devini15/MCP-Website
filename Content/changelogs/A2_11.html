Minecraft Party Alpha 2.11


Additions

  • Removed some commands that would run every tick for optimization reasons
  • Reduced hunter regeneration level in Ghost Hunt (III ? II)
  • Removed base plates from player pawn armor stands

  • Spectator are teleported to the wrong spot for Color Chaos

  • Spectators appear on Whack-A-Mob scoreboard
  • Spectator names appear on screen at end of game
