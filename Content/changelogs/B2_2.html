Minecraft Party Beta 2.2


Additions

  • Non operators are now able to explore the map again
  • Easy-to-read star cost display above Lost City
  • Phantoms have arrived in Dragon Doom
  • Boss Battle now outputs total damage per player in chat at game end
  • New victory room for Floating Islands (now with an audience)
  • Overwriting a trap by placing a 2nd trap atop it is now a toggle in settings
  • 2v2 variant of Meat Slap for doubles mode
  • Players can now wager any amount of coins from 10-40 in addition to existing options
  • Players can use "/trigger Spectate" (even if non-op) to enter spectator mode on the board for better visibility.
  • NEW BOARD: Candyland

Changes

  • Tile Capture now ends automatically if all tiles are captured
  • Hats now remain equipped in the minigame selection room
  • Added a wall to Horse Race to prevent confusion on one of the paths
  • Players are briefly granted resistance in Lost City's guardian boss fight so they can get their bearings.
  • "next_turn" function now removes team tags from spectators
  • Removed death messages from Take Flight
  • Disabled fall damage in Pearl Hurl
  • Put more arrow signs in Pearl Hurl to aid navigation
  • Removed spooky invisible dolphins from guardian boss arena
  • Block Buster no longer locks spectators into one arena in doubles mode
  • Other team losing coins no longer appears on yellow space outcomes if they don't have any (doubles)
  • Restricted spectation box choice in Button Search in doubles mode to prevent ghosting
  • Teammates now always spawn across from each other in Platforms in doubles mode
  • Players now tie if they fail the same round of Color Chaos, even if their deaths are a few ticks apart.
  • Made some lighting changes to Rainbow Run to make the game easier to see at night
  • Modified a cave in Pearl Hurl to make it look more natural
  • Swapped out some items in Port Priority for more familiar ones (no more "guess the shard")

Fixes

  • Eggcellence only awarded one player the win if gold team won in doubles mode
  • Points scoreboard displayed team stars in some minigames (doubles)
  • Floating Islands well looked bad
  • Several typos in quest dialogue
  • Dropper could generate rings partially out of bounds
  • Mineshaft Madness would put lava in the hub
  • One Shot Wonder was rolled multiple times in the same game
  • Floating Islands Racing Star tellraw did not sum laps completed for doubles teams at end of game
  • Players found new and creative ways to not spawn in the right spot before the game starts
  • Deaths did not work correctly in One Shot Wonder
  • Hardini premature ejaculation when players walked above him
  • Hardini used his magic to try to keep you with him after his challenge
  • Same team could appear twice in Chance Time
  • A yellow spot outcome was bad at math (mistitle)
  • Tedious Tasks put spectators on the scoreboard at the end
  • Grammatical error when no players are eligible for a duel
  • One of the teleporters broke down in Floating Islands
  • Removed player mortality from Turtle Hockey
  • Orange space would cap teams at the player item limit instead of the team item limit
  • Players could get trapped in starting hub
  • Podiums did not load correctly for draws
  • One Shot Wonder did not give players arrows on respawn
  • Star Race mode displayed bonus stars in chat even when the corresponding star was disabled
  • Spectating players could escape the board area
