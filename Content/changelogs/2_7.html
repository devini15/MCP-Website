Minecraft Party 2.7


Additions

  • Players can now right-click items to use them on the board
  • Hungry Hungry Humans added to mini-party mode
  • Added trigger objective for spectators exploring the map in the hub for them to return
  • There is another way to get on the roof in Hide and Seek
  • Various dialogue options depending on star cost on Rhythmic Room
  • Large arena is now used for Tanks in Mega Party Mode

Changes

  • World border in What The Cluck? now damages the chickens
  • Wall added in Hog Riders to prevent player from taking the wrong path
  • Laser Evaders first direction includes "ceiling" in addition to floor (referring to how you can get damaged)
  • Players now face away from the Elder Guardian when entering the boss fight (to avoid the instant suffocation damage)
  • Players now ride the chickens in What The Cluck?
  • Dropping feathers now do nothing in Raft Racers when you're riding the raft
  • Increase time for the end roll title from 2 seconds to 3 seconds
  • Decreased time for Mouse Trap for 2v3, 2v4, 2v5 versions
  • Star Cost now changes after each star purchase on RR

Fixes

  • The wrong pig could get killed after a player finishes in Hog Riders
  • Wrong default coin investment amount listed on sign on Suburbia Scramble
  • Quotes showing up on duel signs that get quoted
  • One between space was in a slightly wrong location on SS
  • Missing space between <Yes> and <No> in the challenge dialogue for Mr. P on Center Park
  • Slot machine took away invisibility from the turn player when Ghost Mode (&+) was active on CP
  • "Japanese" misspelled in credits room
  • Grammatical errors on Rick/Nick's chest/barrel shop overview on Lost City
  • Free item pickup trigger did not take a reroll token away from the turn player
  • Players could get stuck by first drum on RR
  • Players could escape the path on RR by one of the drums
  • Minecraft bug failsafe: Eggcellence/Puzzled/Tanks attributes not updating sometimes
  • Speedy Equipment did not upgrade if the player had the full set and had the extra spaces turned off
  • The fence in Market Mayhem was still broken
  • Certain spawnpoints in Recruitment Royale would not allow wither skeletons to escape a section of the map and therefore not be able to get hit in a hole
