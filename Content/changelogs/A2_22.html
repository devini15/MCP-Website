Minecraft Party Alpha 2.22


Additions

  • NEW BOARD: Lost City
  • Audio feedback for equipping a hat in the hat room

Changes

  • Floating chest is now easier to see at night on Port Town
  • Coin score now decrements in real time while using Sacrificial Altar on Floating Islands
  • There are now signs indicating the location of Mr. P and the Slot Machine on Center Park

Fixes

  • Players could sometimes not move from start on Port Town
  • Audience would sometimes appear facing the wrong way after minigames/events
  • Fishing duel would skip itself
  • Players were able to shoot each other in Tanks before the game started
  • Double Dice item failed to roll
  • Double Trouble event did not double coins from team games
  • Audience did not spawn during the special event
  • Audience did not spawn in practice mode on Duel minigames
  • Players got weakness while playing Fight Fight Fight
  • Players coins came back if they viewed the board while using the Sacrificial Altar
  • Floating Islands would not count a lap if it was completed in the same roll as another lap
  • Ready up items were not given in Dragon Doom
  • Dragon Doom would sometimes declare a tie when there was no tie
  • Floor colors stopped changing to reflect teams in the minigame selection room
  • Center Park lottery was a scam (star not awarded despite tickets purchased)
  • Sacrificial Altar did not increment item score correctly upon distributing items
  • Player pawn armor stands are labeled "Armor Stand" instead of the name of the player they represent
  • There was a minor grammatical error in the Slot Machine approach text
  • Flags would sometimes fail to spawn for Capture the Flag
  • Extraneous emerald block was left over from construction in Dragon Doom
  • Ice Trap text in chat did not have a hover event when trap activated
  • Falling off one of the staircases in Hide & Seek would put the player into an inescapable prison
