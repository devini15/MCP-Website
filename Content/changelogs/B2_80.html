Minecraft Party Beta 2.80


Additions

  • New Hub decorations including alternating lights and mirrored blocks in restrooms
  • Nighttime mode in quickplay
  • NEW System for selecting teams, and player/spectator role in teams mode and pairs mode
  • Players can now select their own color!

Changes

  • Temple signs are now translated on Dusty Dunes
  • Players now lose 1 heart when pressing the wrong button in the odd button out section of a temple challenge on DD
  • Monsters now spawn facing the player in Whack-A-Mob
  • Pearls are now TP'd above the slabs when going up water towers in Pearl Hurl
  • Boosts now last longer with high ping mode enabled in Take Flight
  • Amethyst on decorative cactus are now cactus flowers on DD
  • Beds in Market Mayhem are now block displays to prevent villagers from going to them during the night
  • Solo player is slightly faster in Slime Time 1v3
  • Complexity/Star Abundancy signs were copied to the bottom so they're easier to see
  • Star Spawns are prettier on Center Park
  • Victory room is better on CP
  • Trap Overwriting now defaults to off
  • Moved a battle minigame space so it can no longer be reached on the first turn on CP
  • Depending on the amount of players, the (2v2v2...etc.) minigames show the amount on the bottom line of the sign
  • Retargeting functions differently in Boss Battle
  • Tile blocks are now concrete in Tile Capture
  • Moved spot prediction arrows at the altar beacon to be centered from the beacon on Mooshroom Grotto
  • More space for audience in team victory room on MG
  • All items are now killed when a minigame ends
  • Solo player now holds a lead (for decoration) in Ravager Rodeo
