Minecraft Party 3.0 (April Fools)


Additions

  • NEW MINIGAME: Rap Battle (Duel)
  • Each board now has one "tricky space" (a black space that looks like a yellow space)
  • NEW ITEM: Uno Reverse - reverses the turn order (13 coins)
  • NEW ITEM: Gun - Targeted player's rolls are halved for 5 turns (50 coins)
  • "Lizard" and "Spock" items added to Rock Paper Scissors Chase
  • New "Super Chance Time" spaces appear during the last 5 turns
  • Star Stealers are now a rare drop from free item pickups

Changes

  • Increased value of Mooshrooms in Hungry Hungry Humans
  • Removed "Swap Board Spaces" event from Chance Time
  • Communism now has a chance to redistribute Stars instead of coins
  • Blue spaces now have a chance of starting a duel (3 coins will still be given)
  • Lost City star cost now randomizes every turn instead of every 3 turns
  • Removed cutscene fast forward sign from Lost City
  • Added more in-depth explanations to NPC dialogue on Floating Islands for clarity
  • Removed Betris (controls were too confusing)
  • Candyland Trick-or-Treat event now has a very small chance that you just win the whole game
  • Same space duels in the last 5 turns are now best of 3
  • Big Dice can now roll up to 40
  • Horse Race is now 5 laps to allow behind players more time to catch up
  • Creepers in creeper catchers will now explode if they occupy the same space as a player
  • More spaces in Pac Maze to reduce choices made per turn (now 275 spaces)
  • Minigames now happen at the end of every roll instead of every turn

Fixes

  • Rolling [8][6][3] (in that order) on a Thrice Dice would end the game automatically
  • One of the Floating Islands NPCs would say a slur we weren't aware of (sorry!)
  • Turn Skipper made players unreasonably upset
  • Creepers were far too easy to maneuver in Creeper Catchers
  • Players who's IGNs contained the letter "Q" could not roll above 5 (per dice block)
  • Devin's RNG was far too good
