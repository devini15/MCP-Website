Minecraft Party Beta 2.77


Additions

  • New Lobby Music
  • Personal respawn timer in Capture the Flag
  • Various minigames point objectives are now ordered in terms of placement
  • Star Abundancy and Complexity on the main board signs in the hub
  • Players can view Star Abundancy in the corner of the hub when freely viewing boards
  • Board names are now translated

Changes

  • Translated the text displays in front of the altars that listed the items on Mooshroom Grotto
  • Loominardy is now shown as a "Datapack Integrator" in credits hovering over "Translators"
  • During a roll, air is now filled beneath barriers at the dice's location and replaced
  • Players are now enabled on spectate when swapping players
  • Blocks are now cleared slightly outside the clone check region when a player finishes a build in Speed Builders
  • Minigames in minigame selection room are now translated
  • Pass spot event dialogs now show to all players on Zen Garden
  • Moved jukebox so it is less hidden for the music disc challenge on ZG
  • Bars open smoothly in Gladiators
  • Lightning Trap has been renamed to Zap Trap
  • Lightning Storm has been renamed to Lightning Trap
  • Rock Paper Scissors Chase text display is TP'd up more in the minigame disabling room
  • Minion Pet now spawns on the first space on the left path if a player uses the item on the starting space at the start of the game on Airborne Isles
  • Wall is now added each time the rules are read in Take Flight
  • Player pawns are now visible during event cutscenes
  • Gold Team's flag will face players by default in Capture the Flag
  • Players lose the pickaxe if no more emeralds are available on AI
  • Character's feet now move at larger angles in Balance Beam
  • Spectators are now TP'd one block above center when the player_line function runs to ensure they aren't viewing the inside of a player's head

  • When a player landed on a space with two opponents, the turn player was not able to choose the wager for a Micro Duel

  • Bat Blast did not award coins

  • Item skulls were missing on free item pickups on ZG
  • Pellets would float in the air in Pac Cube

  • Turn player's name was missing in chat when forging Star Shards
  • Reroll token wouldn't clear after rolling a 0
  • The location of spot prediction arrows going down into the mesa cave island were bad on AI
  • There was no drink in the chest for the drink quest on AI
  • Players would not receive their equipment on them (but would still keep their abilities) at the start of the first player's turn
  • The brown mooshroom mysteriously changed to red thus making the suspicious stew quest impossible on AI
