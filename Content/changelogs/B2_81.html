Minecraft Party Beta 2.81


Additions

  • New Bonus Star: Red Star

Changes

  • Much easier to get onto the roofs of houses in Block Buster
  • Not power color is now white in Cannoneers

  • When attempting to swap places with a minion, a change message appears depending on whether the player has passed the space they are standing on
