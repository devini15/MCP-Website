Minecraft Party Beta 2.26


Additions

  • Warp Maze challenge (PC) displays message while loading
  • Grand Prix mode 6 player compatibility
  • Grand Prix mode 10 player compatibility
  • Audio feedback when blasting a bat in Bat Blast
  • NEW ITEM: [S] Honey Zoom
  • NEW ITEM: [S] Star Setter
  • NEW ITEM: [S] Coin Stealer

Changes

  • Updated preview diorama of Pipe Canyon to match wall material

  • Quickplay now handles disconnections correctly in real time
  • Quest boards no longer give the players the quest barrel during the star ceremony

  • If the doubles_mode variable was unset, some 1v3 minigames would be unavailable
  • Erroneous barrier was present on Floating Islands
  • Spelling error in sacrificial enchantment shard lore
  • Eye of ender not removed from quest barrel if players were in spectator mode when it should have been
  • Players were unable to cancel lightning traps
  • Traps indicators would sometimes get pushed away from the center of their space
  • Coins could be used to craft Yellow Dye
  • Floating Islands stew quest did not update correctly
  • Incorrect jingle played when rolling weighted twice/thrice dice
  • Players were able to discard their board items during the special event ceremony
  • Players were able to make the dandelion from the FI stew quest into dye
  • Raw steak from FI steak quest goes bad too quickly if you have too many friends
  • Spirit Shields could not be discarded
  • Mooshroom Grotto Star Altar would never give out Stars or Triple Star Vouchers
