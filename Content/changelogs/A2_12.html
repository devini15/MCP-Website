Minecraft Party Alpha 2.12


Additions

  • Big Dice now have a unique texture
  • Added custom player head for Pac Cube

Changes

  • Teleport to Turn Player no longer allows you to continually warp inside the player
  • Player health is now set to full at the start of Treetop Hop
  • Removed wall in pond area of Floating Islands; Replaced with barriers.

Fixes

  • You can die in Team Hockey
  • Spectators appear on scoreboard in Whack-a-Mob
  • Spectators get wrong player scores for coin minigames
  • Spectator lives appear on scoreboard in Knockout
  • Spectator scores appear in Rainbow Run
  • Barriers prevent players from moving correctly in some places on Floating Islands
  • Some teleports do not apply to spectators correctly
  • Black spaces will appear to put you in debt in some cases
  • Spectators get coins from black space events
  • Spectators appear on scoreboard when stars are swapped in Chance Time
  • Spectators can "Win" "Diamonds" in Platforms
  • One of the Lamps on Floating Islands does not turn on at night
  • Star Seller will sometimes face the wrong direction
  • Speed Builders title text is the wrong color
  • Players sometimes get sent to purgatory instead of Horse Race
  • Fishing Quest on Floating Islands is impossible
  • Spectator lap score shared in ending ceremony
