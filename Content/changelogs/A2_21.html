Minecraft Party Alpha 2.21


Additions

  • NEW MINIGAME: Eggcellence
  • NEW MINIGAME: Dragon Doom
  • New build as a location for various events (Roll Off, Chance Time, etc.)
  • Very big wall at end of Rocket Launch field (let's see vivi fly over this one)

Changes

  • Reduced radius of hub forcing adventure mode to prevent it dropping you into oblivion
  • Players now have saturation instead of weakness in the hub (punching enabled)
  • Increased loading time before playing Port Town to ensure board loads correctly
  • Rainbow Run now gives more information on streak bonus in the rules
  • Cave Parkour now ends automatically once 3 players have finished
  • Reduced seek time by 15 seconds in Hide & Seek
  • Reduced Horse Race time limit by 30 seconds
  • Chance time spaces are now prettier
  • Increased the health of the single player in Boss Brawl
  • Serving now sends the ball higher in Deuce
  • Implemented new textures for Twice Dice and Thrice Dice

Fixes

  • Temporary garbage would spawn underneath the permanent garbage in Betris
  • Flying Chest challenge difficulty was higher than intended on Port Town
  • Pig Pushers occasionally listed multiple winners (including non-participants)
  • Mineshaft Madness would steal your pickaxe upon being replayed
  • Spectators not teleported to Ring in the Ring arena
  • Players could attack each other in Ring in the Ring
  • Distance cap in Rocket Launch was not enforced properly
  • Roll off winnings were not affected by double trouble
