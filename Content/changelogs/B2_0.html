Minecraft Party Beta 2.0


Additions

  • NEW GAMEMODE: DOUBLES
  • Space count now displays on board preview signs
  • Guardian boss fight on Lost City now shows player health to spectators
  • Added the most messed up thing we could think of to Guardian boss fight
  • Lost City now allows you to skip through star price cutscene
  • Visual roulette when deducing battle minigame wagers
  • There is now a lighthouse on Port Town that gives players the ability to steal coins
  • More robust foundations on the islands of Floating Islands
  • Chat announces when you get eliminated in Spleef
  • NEW MINIGAME: Tip Drop (1v3)

Changes

  • Settings sign in hub is now bold for better visibility
  • P1 no longer glows during Lost City's star price cutscene
  • Boat Race renamed to "Raft Racers"
  • Raft Racers chests replaced with baby rabbits to reduce collision detector size
  • Naughty Spectators now get cunked in Hardini's challenge
  • Labyrinth traps no longer trigger if both players occupy the trapped tile
  • Reduced sound volume when landing on black space
  • Implemented some optimizations for functions that run every tick
  • Effect removal implemented more cleanly
  • Magma and fire blocks removed from the Knockout terrain
  • Prison Pals wool pattern is now translated in a different plane to make the directions clearer
  • Replaced Port Town Portal with blackstone island

Fixes

  • Dice were not killed on game cancellation, which could cause buggy behavior
  • Spectators could see inside the chests in the key shop during Hardini's challenge
  • Minor punctuation error in Gladiators instructions
  • Both teams scoring a goal on the same tick in Team Hockey would softlock the minigame
  • Mismatched title in yellow space roulette
  • Closed shops still appeared open above the board
  • Re-removed 2 pesky cobwebs that could be used to cheese Block Buster
  • You could throw out your meat in Meat Slap
  • Troll toll didn't work properly when swapping players
