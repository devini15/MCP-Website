Minecraft Party Beta 2.1


Additions

  • NPCs now present in Floating Islands shops
  • Doubles functionality added to Star Race mode
  • Failsafe slimeball to prevent softlocking in battle minigames when a player disconnects
  • Dialogue when approaching a shop or ticket master on Center Park
  • Quest item syncing for doubles on Floating Islands
  • Light blocks in Center Park victory room
  • NEW MINIGAME: One Shot Wonder
  • Added armor stands for several playtesters to audience

Changes

  • Extended 1v3 branch of minigame selection to capacitate more games
  • Max items setting now works better with doubles mode
  • Relevant signs in settings now specify "per player" or "per team" in doubles mode
  • If a bonus star is tied in doubles, neither team is awarded the star
  • Changed the official name of First to # Stars mode to Star Race
  • Tip Drop is now called Dripstone Dance
  • Ghost Mode now allows tax evasion (immunity to Coin Thief)
  • Star Sellers can no longer spawn in the same "loop" on Center Park
  • Swapped the theming of the Battle and Duel game selection rooms
  • Replaced glass panes in Rock, Paper, Scissors, Chase to prevent players from getting stuck on the spectation window
  • Players are now ejected further if they attempt to step on the court on their serve in Deuce
  • Ghost mode now tries harder to convince you you're a ghost

Fixes

  • Armor stands on the board displayed their team name twice in doubles mode
  • Doubles mode did not update coins correctly if your teammate was offline when they were collected
  • Battle minigame wagers were deducted incorrectly in doubles mode
  • Swapping out players did not maintain the doubles team of the swapped player
  • Bew feared the law (won't steal coins in Port Town)
  • Players had resistance in Snow Wars
  • Players had resistance in Tanks
  • Single ues star sellers didn't glow at first
  • There was a hole in the barriers above Port Town
  • Eggcelence build not updated correctly
  • Eggcelence failed to teleport spectators
  • Eggcelence declared 1 player the winner, despite being a 2v2
  • Viewing the board after using a Twice or Thrice Dice would add an extra dice block to your inventory
  • Doubles team star counts no longer said "Stars" on the scoreboard after playing Team Hockey
  • Tying for a bonus star caused scoreboard issues in doubles
  • Insufficient loading time provided for Dripstone Dance
  • Bonus star announcement was missing the word "with" between team name and score in doubles
  • Bonus star ceremony would call your team a "player" in doubles
  • OP Stats book did not render properly after doubles
  • Team Hockey would spawn a bonus turtle after the 2nd point was scored
  • Doubles teams could gain coins by landing on a red space with 0 coins
  • Black space event sometimes gets the number of coins wrong
  • Chance time occasionally failed to swap players positions
  • Extraneous space in Chance Time message for giving coins
  • Spectators got team tags at doubles ceremony (this caused other issues)
  • Starting Quickplay in doubles mode would cause issues when attempting to play 1v3 minigames.
