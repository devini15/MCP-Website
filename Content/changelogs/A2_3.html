Minecraft Party Alpha 2.3


Additions

  • Crazy Clock now has item description in it's lore
  • New larger arena for In The Zone
  • Visual for power-up respawn timer in Pac-Cube
  • New Minigame: Tunnel o' Fun
  • New Minigame: Creeper Catchers
  • New Minigame: Take Flight
  • Return of spleef
  • New, optimized Betris music
  • New Green Space Event: Roll Off
  • Added Fight Fight Fight duel (Player Fight from MCP1)

Changes

  • There are now trade limits in Market Mayhem
  • Removed Coin Shower event
  • Players must now sprint the entire time while playing Balance Beam in High Ping mode
  • Voiding a minigame no longer requires all 4 players to be present
  • Changed the colors of the default signs in the settings room
  • Market Mayhem rules now specify that chests restock
  • Boat no longer has free movement before Tanks starts
  • Added an arrow to Cave Parkour to clarify a confusing route
  • You can no longer drop your pearls in Pearl Hurl
  • Removed misleading arrows in Port Priority
  • In the Zone no longer has end portal walls
  • The solo player now gets Knockback 4 in In The Zone (buff)
  • The solo player now regenerates faster in Ghost Hunt
  • Solo player's sword does 1 more damage in Ghost Hunt
  • Slightly reduced solo player speed in Slime Time
  • Betris is no longer omitted from High Ping mode
  • Arrows now take longer to come back in Boss Brawl
  • Removed water from Boss Brawl arena
  • You can now change your mind on who to fight before starting a duel
  • Team selection room now has signs specifying roles in quickplay mode
  • You now return to the board, even if a player gets disconnected when the minigame ends
  • Reduced duration of power-up effects in Slime Time
  • Rearranged some code to optimize functions that run every tick
  • Mineshaft Madness now ends if all players are dead

Fixes

  • Voiding Maze Navigator too early breaks things
  • 1st lava layer is actually 2 layers in Mineshaft Madness
  • Coin piles do not reappear on board after minigames
  • Cheese does not load correctly in Betris
  • Last player found in Block Buster gets stuck in the shadow realm (spectator mode)
  • Tanks does not end correctly
  • Tanks does not void correctly
  • Port Priority doesn't clear your inventory when the game ends
