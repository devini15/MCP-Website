Minecraft Party Alpha 2.7


Additions

  • Realms mode (disables minigames in custom dimensions)
  • Minigame practice is available before playing the real game
  • Sounds for killing mobs in Whack-a-Mob
  • Brief cutscene when deciding what will happen on a Green Space
  • Arrow skulls added to intersections on boards
  • More playtesters added to final victory area
  • Swap player scores function allows players to be switched out if need be
  • New Minigame: Deuce

Changes

  • Less gold spawns on the bottom belt in Gold Rush
  • Removed water at end of Rocket Launch field
  • Changed some text coloring in Steve Says
  • Dropper now ends automatically if 3 players reach the bottom
  • Blazes are now 1 hit kills in Whack-a-Mob
  • Modified some text in Floating Islands to be more concise
  • Modified rules in Block Buster
  • Nerfed remaining brown mushrooms in Treetop Hop
  • Changed the way some instructions are worded in Prison Pals
  • Block Buster is now 60 seconds shorter
  • Hunter now receives hint as to hider proximity in Block Buster
  • Hiders can now move around in Block Buster after the hunter is released
  • There is now a chat message when you catch a fish in Center Park
  • Spectators can now get onto the corners of the ring in Ring in the Ring

Fixes

  • Tunnel O Fun does not generate a tunnel in which to have fun
  • Beacons and items sometimes fail to appear in Port Priority
  • Spectators can shoot each other in Tanks
  • Axe does the wrong amount of damage in Boss Brawl
  • Crazy Clock interrupts duels for landing on players if used in last 5 turns
  • Duels sometimes don't activate when landing on another player in the last 5 turns
  • Intersection arrows to not render properly after warping in Center Park
