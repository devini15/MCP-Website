Minecraft Party Beta 2.3


Additions

  • New sounds for Twice Dice and Thrice Dice items
  • Arrows to indicate legal movement directions in Candyland
  • Added free item spaced to Candyland
  • New setting to have special event determined by a roulette
  • 2v2s that are based on 1v3s can now be accessed in Quickplay by enabling doubles mode
  • Added free item spaces to Floating Islands
  • NEW BOARD: Pac-Maze

Changes

  • Orange spaces now allow the player to choose an item to discard if their inventory is full.
  • Streamlined the dialogue options for buying a star on Candyland
  • Runner no longer kills players instantly for standing still in High Ping mode
  • Free item spaces can now give Ender Traps on Candyland
  • Settings are now preserved between games
  • Additional minigame coins from double trouble are no longer counted towards the All Star
  • Players are now allowed one mulligan if they botch the serve in Deuce
  • Lone player now gets speed in Dripstone Dance

Fixes

  • One Shot Wonder did not handle deaths properly
  • Failing the Gingerbread challenge would softlock the game
  • Spectators were barred from entering the Duel, Chance Time, or Roll Off rooms
  • Players could escape Candyland by climbing over the wall in adventure mode
  • Double Star Voucher did not sync correctly to the team inventory in doubles
  • Ender trap did not update item counts correctly in doubles mode
  • Extraneous "." in Ender Trap hover event
  • Players could trigger traps left by their teammate in doubles
  • Star seller did not reset the voucher trade correctly
  • Player 1 could not trick or treat on their 1st turn on Candyland
  • Sometimes, players would game over before Rocket Launch even started
  • Players were able to access the duel initiation platform when they were not supposed to
  • Settings would reset upon exiting a board preview
  • Orange space item discard did not work on Center Park, Port Town, or Floating Islands
  • Using '/trigger Spectate' before turn order was decided would break the game
