The Devini15 Open

Rules & Settings

WORK IN PROGRESS
  • 20 Turns
  • Equipment disabled
  • All Minigames
    • If both teams agree they do not wish to play a minigame, they may petition event organizers to disable it.
  • Each team will have their own voice channel they are expected to be in for most of the game.
  • No trap overwriting
  • Vanilla Chance Time
  • Set bonus stars (TBD)
  • Turn Skippers cost 5 Coins but skip the turn of a random player
    • Note that this could be the player using the item or their teammate
  • No Star Protectors/Safes
  • Quarter & Semifinals are 3 game matches (FT2)
  • Grand Final is a 5 game match (FT3)
  • Boards for matches will be selected via a round of Picks & Bans
  • "+5 Chance Time Spaces" removed from Last 5 Turns event pool
  • Servers for tournament games will be closed to spectators.
    • Anyone wishing to watch the game can catch the official cast!
  • Team item inventory size = 5
  • Max items/turn = 1

Structure

  • Each team will select 1 representative to participate in a 10-turn 10-player Mega PartyTM qualifier game.
    • 1 team will be designated as the "Odd Team Out" and will not participate in this qualifier.
  • The teams of the players who come in 9th and 10th in the qualifier will play 1 board for 10th place
  • The team of the player who came in 8th in the qualifier will play 1 board against the Odd Team Out for 8th seed
  • The remaining teams will all be placed in the quarterfinal bracket seeded based on qualifier placement
