The Devini15 Open

Rules & Settings

  • 20 Turns
  • Equipment disabled
  • Enhanced Items disabled
  • All Minigames
    • If both teams agree they do not wish to play a minigame, they may petition event organizers to disable it.
  • Each team will have their own voice channel they are expected to be in for most of the game.
  • No trap overwriting
  • Vanilla Chance Time
  • Set bonus stars
    • Coin Star
    • Event Star
    • Board specific star
  • Turn Skippers cost 5 Coins but skip the turn of a random player
    • Note that this could be the player using the item or their teammate
  • No Star Protectors/Safes
  • Quarter & Semifinals are 3 game matches (FT2)
  • Grand Final is a 5 game match (FT3)
  • Boards for matches will be selected via a round of Picks & Bans
  • Team item inventory size = 5
  • Max items/turn = 1

Conduct

  • Be respectful of opponents and hosts
  • No Auto-Clickers
  • No Screenshots
  • No Macros
  • No Mods Except:
    • Fabric Loader
    • Optifine
    • Sodium
    • WI Zoom
  • No Random Number Generators
  • No Use of a notepad/spreadsheet/external document/tool of any kind to track stats during a game.

Content

  • Participants are welcome and encouraged to record their PoV, but kindly do not live stream the event.
  • Servers for tournament games will be closed to spectators.
    • Anyone wishing to watch the game can catch the official cast!

Structure

  • Each team will compete in a single 10-turn doubles "seeding" game against Devin & John on Port Town.
  • Teams will be seeded based on relative performance in the seeding game.
  • The top 5 seeded teams automatically qualify for quarterfinals.
  • The remaining teams will participate in a preliminary round of 1 board games as per the bracket linked below.

Scheduling

  • Teams will be expected to schedule their next game within 1 week of its announcement.
  • Difficulties with scheduling will be handled on a case-by-case basis.
  • If a team is unresponsive or unavailable, they may be forced to forfeit at hosts' discretion.
