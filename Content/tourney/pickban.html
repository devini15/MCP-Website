Pick & Ban Order

Picks and bans will be live and in order. Unlike the "blind ban" system in previous tournaments, teams will know immediately what their opponents bans and picks are, and will be able to directly respond to them.

KEY:
Ban - Ban a board from being played this match.
Pick - Select a board to be played this match.
Decider - Randomly selected board.
Aqua: Higher seeded team.
Gold: Lower seeded team.
Green: Selected at random.
